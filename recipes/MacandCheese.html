MAC&CHEESE

Description

To craft a classic and indulgent macaroni and cheese, begin by boiling a pot of water and cooking elbow macaroni until it reaches a tender yet slightly firm texture. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and stir in an equal amount of all-purpose flour to create a roux. Gradually whisk in warm milk, ensuring a smooth consistency. As the mixture thickens, add generous amounts of shredded sharp cheddar cheese, stirring until the cheese fully melts into the velvety sauce. Season with a pinch of salt and a dash of mustard for an extra depth of flavor. Combine the luscious cheese sauce with the cooked macaroni, coating each piece evenly in the rich mixture.

For the finishing touch, transfer the creamy macaroni and cheese into a baking dish. Top it with additional shredded cheese and, if desired, breadcrumbs for a delightful crunch. Bake the dish in a preheated oven until the top becomes golden brown and bubbly. The aroma of bubbling cheese and the anticipation of that golden crust will heighten your senses. Once out of the oven, let it cool for a few minutes before serving, allowing the flavors to meld together. Spoon out a portion of this comforting and cheesy delight onto your plate, savoring each forkful of the creamy macaroni and cheese, a timeless and irresistible dish for all ages.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Warm milk
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Mustard
  • Breadcrumbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook elbow macaroni until tender.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan and stir in flour to create a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in warm milk for a smooth consistency.
  4. Add shredded sharp cheddar cheese, stirring until melted.
  5. Season with salt and a dash of mustard for flavor.
  6. Combine cheese sauce with cooked macaroni, coating evenly.
  7. Transfer mixture to a baking dish.
  8. Top with additional shredded cheese and breadcrumbs.
  9. Bake until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  10. Cool for a few minutes before serving and enjoy!
